DAT (Diploma in Accounting and Taxation)

DAT (Diploma in Accounting and Taxation) course meets the industry standards. The course curriculum covers basic to advanced accounting, Income Tax & TDs, Tally ERP 9 with GST, and Auditing.

Advance Tally 4.0

The Advance Tally 4.0 course provides foundational knowledge in accounting principles and bookkeeping techniques, preparing individuals for entry-level roles in accounting firms, corporations, or government agencies, with a focus on financial statements, payroll procedures, and regulatory compliance.

Practical Accountant

Explore your 11th and 12th Standard syllabus practically with our Practical Account course. Dive into Accounting and Bookkeeping using Tally and other software, ensuring hands-on learning every day for 2 hours, including both practical and theory sessions.

SAP Financial Accounting

The SAP Financial Accounting (SAP FI) Training Course is tailored to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills essential for managing financial accounting processes within SAP's comprehensive ERP system.

SAP Human Capital Management

Unlock the potential of SAP Human Capital Management (HCM) with our comprehensive training course.Designed for aspiring HR professionals and seasoned practitioners alike, this course equips individuals with the essential skills and knowledge required to effectively manage human resources within organizations using SAP HCM software.

ABAP(Advanced Business Application Programming)

Our SAP ABAP Training course is meticulously designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience in SAP Advanced Business Application Programming (ABAP). ABAP serves as the primary programming language within the SAP ecosystem, empowering developers to create custom applications, reports, and enhancements.

Digital Marketing

A digital marketing course covers essential strategies and tools for online promotion, including SEO, social media, email marketing, and content creation. It teaches how to analyze digital metrics and optimize campaigns to reach target audiences effectively.

Full Stack Developer

A full stack developer course provides comprehensive training in both front-end and back-end web development. It covers languages and frameworks such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and databases like MongoDB or SQL, equipping students with the skills to build and maintain complete web applications.
